PROJECT DESCRIPTION
The work consists of replacing approximately 740 linear feet of failing 18-inch
corrugated metal pipe (CMP) culverts at three locations along Bowman Road, plus
4,200 cubic yards of channel grading along Cottonwood Creek south of the Gas
Point Road bridge. Work includes mobilization, traffic control, clearing and
grubbing, removal of existing culverts, structural excavation, placement of new
HDPE culverts with concrete headwalls, riprap erosion protection, asphalt patch
paving, and final striping.
